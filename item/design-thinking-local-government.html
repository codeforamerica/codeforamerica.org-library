Design Thinking & Local Government

Designing for Residents

Contributors

Cyd Harrell, Brian Yeung, Cris Cristina, Jonathan Rewers, Dana Chisnell.

Tags

Design, Panel Discussion, UX.

Programs

Peer Network.

Locations

Boston, MA, Oakland, CA, San Francisco, CA.

Date

  • 2013

Link: http://www.codeforamerica.org/05-30-2013/

This roundtable discussion is about how to use design thinking to build better government services. Building a great citizen experience requires understanding citizens as users and then designing government processes that respect their time, dignity, and abilities. In this session, we focus on sharing lightweight techniques you can implement to design online and offline services that invite full participation by citizens; and rebuild their trust in government. This panel includes user experience design experts from both public and private sector backgrounds.

This session originally aired on May 30, 2013.
